Research Vision

My vision is to harness the power of artificial intelligence to accelerate scientific discovery, reshape the future of research, and catalyze a new era of scientific revolutions and groundbreaking innovations. Currently, my focus lies at AI for Molecule Science, with the aim of making a significant impact on research across biology, materials science, pharmaceuticals, and chemistry.

My current research interests are centered on:

  • 1. Deciphering the Language of Molecules. Developing physics-informed molecular foundation models that capture universal representations, unlocking deeper insights into molecular behavior and interactions.
  • 2. Revolutionizing Molecular Design. Using generative models to accelerate the design of novel materials and drugs, enhancing the efficiency, precision, and scalability of discovery in pharmaceutical and materials research.
  • 3. General Algorithmic Breakthroughs. Exploring how AI advancements in molecular science can inspire and inform the development of novel AI algorithms with broader applications.
