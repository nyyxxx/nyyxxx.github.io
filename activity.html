Presentation at Renmin University

Dec 2024 | Beijing

Invited to present our group's work on AI4Science and spoke after Professor Lan as a representative of our lab student. Meeting peers and experts in the field made me excited to imagine the future AI4S might help create.

Zhongguancun International Youth Forum

Dec 2024 | Beijing

Attended the forum and shared my research with early-career scholars from around the world. Motivated greatly from constructive discussions and meeting senior researchers I deeply admire.

Doctoral Forum

Dec 2024 | Shanghai

Participated in the joint doctoral forum between Fudan University and Tsinghua University. Delivered two short presentations on AI applications and had enriching exchanges with faculty members. Honored to receive the First Prize in the sub-forum.

ICLR Conference

May 2024 | Vienna, Austria

Attended my first in-person AI conference and presented a poster. Seeing so many leading researchers in person was both thrilling and humbling. Grateful for the encouraging feedback and motivated to pursue more solid and impactful work.

Atom Lab Celebration

2023 | Beijing

Our lab has a sweet tradition: cake for every good thing that happens. These small celebrations make me feel truly at home here — surrounded by people who share the same passion and joy for science. Deeply grateful to Professor Lan for this warm family.

Opening Ceremony Speech

Sep 2023 | Beijing

Honored to deliver a speech as the student representative at the opening ceremony, sharing reflections on research experiences and academic growth.

iGEM Giant Jamboree

Oct 2018 | Boston, MA

This was my first real research project from start to finish. Winning a Gold Medal and Best Track Award was unforgettable, but what mattered more was the fire for scientific research it lit in me.

The choir's professional training gave me a real foundation in vocal technique and musical artistry. Demanding, beautiful, and deeply fulfilling.
